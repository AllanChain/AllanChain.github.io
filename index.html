About

I am Yubing Qian, and my ID on GitHub is AllanChain.

Currently, I am going to be a PhD student at Institute of Condensed Matter and Material Physics, School of Physics, Peking University. I am working on computational physics in Ji Chen's group, mainly interested in quantum Monte Carlo (QMC) with neural networks. I am also a intern at ByteDance Research, focusing on the same topic.

I'm a programming hobbyist and open source lover. My primary programming languages are Python and JavaScript. You can read more on my blog and my GitHub profile.
